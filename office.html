What is Microsoft 365?

Microsoft 365 is a subscription service that ensures you always have the most up-to-date modern productivity tools from Microsoft. It combines the best-in-class productivity of Office apps with intelligent cloud services and world-class security to help you achieve more.

What is the difference between Microsoft 365 and Office 2021?

Microsoft 365 is a subscription service that ensures you always have the most up-to-date modern productivity tools from Microsoft. It combines the best-in-class productivity of Office apps with intelligent cloud services and world-class security to help you achieve more. Office 2021 is a one-time purchase that includes classic Office apps like Word, Excel, and PowerPoint for PC or Mac, but does not include any of the services that come with a Microsoft 365 subscription.

What do I get with a Microsoft 365 subscription?

With a Microsoft 365 subscription, you get the latest premium versions of Word , Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, and more. You also get 1 TB of OneDrive cloud storage per user, advanced security features to help protect your data and devices, and ongoing technical support at no extra cost. Depending on your subscription plan, you may also get additional apps and services such as Microsoft Teams, SharePoint, and Exchange.

Can I install Microsoft 365 on multiple devices?

Yes, with most Microsoft 365 subscription plans, you can install the Office apps on multiple devices, including PCs, Macs, tablets, and smartphones. The number of devices you can install Microsoft 365 on depends on your subscription plan. For example, with a Microsoft 365 Family subscription, you can share your subscription with up to five other people, and each person can install the Office apps on all their devices and be signed in to five devices at the same time. With a Microsoft 365 Personal subscription, you can install the Office apps on all your devices and be signed in to five devices at the same time.

Is an internet connection required to use Microsoft 365?

An internet connection is required to download and install Microsoft 365, manage your subscription, and access certain features and services. However, once the Office apps are installed on your device, you can use them offline without an internet connection. Any changes you make while offline will be synced to the cloud the next time you connect to the internet.
